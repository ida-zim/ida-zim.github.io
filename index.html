About Us

IDA was founded in 2021 by graduate civil engineers who have a strong belief that they can immensely contribute to the alleviation of the effects of natural disasters within their communities. It is a non-profit organisation.

Vision
We imagine a world where people and infrastructure are protected from natural disasters through initiatives that include flood and drought proofing, forecasting and effect-alleviation.
Mission
To alleviate the effects of natural disasters in communities.
